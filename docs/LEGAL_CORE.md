# Uni Folia Legal Core: Structure & Disclosures

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This document is a **Draft for Legal Review**. It must be audited by a qualified professional before final public deployment.

## 1. Service Philosophy & Admissibility
Uni Folia is an **AI-Assisted Educational Drafting Workspace**. Our core commitment is to **provide evidence-grounded, non-fabricated support** for student self-reflection and academic reporting.

- **Authorship Disclosure**: The student user is the unique and final author of any document generated on this platform. Uni Folia acts as a "logic-mirror" and "structure-advisor."
- **No Guarantee of Admission**: Uni Folia does not guarantee admission to any university or program. Admission results depend on the totality of a student's high school record and the independent evaluation of university admissions officers.
- **Fact Integrity Policy**: The platform uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to ground advice in real academic trends and student records. However, users are responsible for verifying all factual claims.

## 2. AI Processing Disclosures (Privacy Policy)
In compliance with global and local AI transparency standards:
- **Data Usage for Inference**: To provide personalized guidance, student records (grades, activities, notes) are temporarily processed by LLM (Large Language Model) providers via secure API.
- **Non-Training Guarantee**: By default, student data sent to Uni Folia's enterprise-grade AI partners (e.g., Google Gemini Vertex AI) is **NOT** used to train the base foundation models.
- **Human-in-the-Loop**: All AI-proposed visuals and text blocks require explicit student approval before they are finalized, ensuring human control over the "black box."

## 3. Support Channels
- **B2C (Student/Parent)**: General usage support and technical troubleshooting.
- **B2B (Schools/Institutions)**: Integration support, administrative dashboards, and institutional licensing inquiries.

## 4. Key Terms for TOS
- **Intellectual Property**: Any original text written by the student remains 100% the property of the student. AI-generated suggestions approved by the student are licensed to the student for their personal academic use.
- **Fair Use**: Users agree not to use Uni Folia to generate purposefully false or fraudulent claims in their academic records.
