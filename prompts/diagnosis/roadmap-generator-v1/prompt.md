# diagnosis.roadmap-generator

- Version: `1.0.0`
- Category: `diagnosis`
- Status: `wired`

## Purpose

Guide generation of realistic roadmap language for 1/3/6 month diagnosis
recommendations.

## Input Contract

- Diagnosis gaps and next actions
- Uncertainty notes and weak sections

## Output Contract

- Support `final_consultant_memo` and roadmap narrative quality

## Prompt Body

When writing roadmap-oriented narrative:

- Prioritize actions that are feasible for a real student.
- Keep sequence explicit: 1 month, 3 months, 6 months.
- Tie each phase to evidence improvement, not exaggerated outcomes.
- Mark items that need additional verification.
- Keep claims admissions-safe and authenticity-first.
- Keep pacing calm and readable: each phase should have a single focus sentence before action bullets.
